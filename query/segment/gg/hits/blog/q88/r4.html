Ruth Rendell
Ruth Rendell (1930) is the most famous crime writer in the world.
She lives in London. She has received many awards for her books.
She started writing novels in 1964. Chief Inspector Wexford is a famous character.
She also writes as 'Barbara Vine'.

This is a Ruth Rendell website where you can find information about her books and her life.
