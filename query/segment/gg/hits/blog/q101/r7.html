George Clooney and his new lady, Stacy Keibler, have taken their budding romance public, stepping out together on red carpet after red carpet, but now they've taken their love south.

They were spotted in Los Cabos, Mexico over the weekend having a relaxing and romantic getaway. George drove his lady around the resort in a golf cart and Bauer Griffin photographers snapped photos of the pair as they had drinks at an outdoor bar.

Stacy, 32, seems quite smitten with her older man. She was all smiles as the duo dined out and took a stroll together, where they couldn't keep their hands off each other.

Here are some photos from their Mexican holiday, head over to Bauer Griffin for some more.
