  • Description: Designed by Frank O. Gehry & located in the city of Bilbao in northern Spain, the museum is devoted to American & European art of the 20th century. It is envisaged as an international center of modern & contemporary art, & will extend the Guggenheim Museum's efforts to bring its collections & programming to audiences around the world. The expressionist postmodern architecture mainly consists of titanium interlocking plates, glass, stainless & stone.
