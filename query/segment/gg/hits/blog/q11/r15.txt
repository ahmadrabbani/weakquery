Also known as Fat Tuesday, Shrove Tuesday, or Pancake Day, Mardi Gras is the final day of the Carnival season. Tomorrow will be Ash Wednesday, the beginning of Lent, so all partying and temptations must be removed, or consumed, by midnight. Here are six ways to celebrate online.       See a Parade  There are plenty of them, from January 26th through today. See some pictures and read about parades  today at Mental Floss . Some video links available.  Drink  Storm of the Century Hurricane * 1 cup ice cubes * 1 fluid ounce light rum * 1 fluid ounce dark rum * 1 fluid ounce coconut rum * 1 fluid ounce vodka * 1 fluid ounce gin * 1 fluid ounce Chambord (raspberry liqueur) * 1 fluid ounce triple sec liqueur * 3 fluid ounces orange juice * orange slices for garnish (optional) * maraschino cherry for garnish (optional) Fill a hurricane glass with ice. Add light rum, dark rum, coconut rum, vodka, gin, raspberry liqueur, triple sec and orange juice. Shake gently, and garnish with orange slices and a cherry, if desired. Beverage will be about the color of a pink grapefruit. More Mardi Gras drinks at  All Recipes .  Eat  Start out the day with  pancakes . Then move on to  shrimp, crawfish, gumbo, and jambalaya . For dessert, its gotta be  King Cake ! What really matters here is that you overindulge.   Wear an Outrageous Costume    More Mardi Gras costumes:  Here and  here and  here . Collect Beads  Get your virtual Mardi Gras beads at  Jokes Unlimited .  I shouted "Throw Me Sumpthin' Mister!" and got  Kiss Me Beads  Get Your Virtual Mardi Gras Beads at JokesUnlimited.com         “Throw me something, Mister!” might get you throws, but flashing will get you more. The  “show me something for beads” tradition is pretty much limited to the French Quarter of New Orleans, and peaked during the 80s and nineties. But you can still see women who are on their way to inebriation giving a flash  for beads , for  attention , or for  no reason at all . Links NSFW.
