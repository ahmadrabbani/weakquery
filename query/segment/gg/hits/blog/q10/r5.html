'Arrested Development' to Return

Dream come true? Cruel joke? Or horrible decision? At the New Yorker Festival Sunday, Arrested Development creator Mitch Hurwitz announced that he intends to make one last “limited” season of the show before filming the movie. The enormously popular show lasted for three seasons on Fox before being cancelled in 2006. Rumors of a film based on the movie have elicited everything from cheers to boos from the most ardent of fans. The cast of the sitcom, including Will Arnett, Jason Bateman, Michael Cera, and Portia de Rossi appeared in “A Bluth Family Reunion” event at the New Yorker Festival.
