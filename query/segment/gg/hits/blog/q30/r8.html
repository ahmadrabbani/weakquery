Senator Harry Reid Lies About The Federal Deficit (Video)

Via The Gateway Pundit, Senator Harry Reid, the U.S. Senate Majority Leader, took to the Senate floor to lecture his fellow Senators that they needed to act on the deficit by increasing taxes.

Senator Reid then proceeded to lie:
"It's time we return to the type of fiscal discipline that democrats brought to Washington in the 90's when democrats in the Congress and White House balanced the budget and used the surplus to do what Mr. President? To pay down the debt."
We have covered this lie about the surplus which Jim Moran told last September here, here, and here. truth is, it was Republicans NOT Democrats who balanced the budget in the 90's.
