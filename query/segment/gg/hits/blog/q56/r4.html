Teri Hatcher

actress
Born: 12/8/1964
Birthplace: Sunnyvale, California

Until 2004 when she became one of ABC's Desperate Housewives (for which she won a Golden Globe), Hatcher was best known for her portrayal of Lois Lane in the television series, Lois & Clark: The New Adventures of Superman (1993–97). Hatcher has shown some comedic talent in movies such as Soap Dish (1991), and more recently was the complicitous co-conspirator in 2 Days in the Valley (1996), the new Bond girl opposite Pierce Brosnan in Tomorrow Never Dies (1997) and took a frivolous turn in kid flick Spy Kids (2001).
