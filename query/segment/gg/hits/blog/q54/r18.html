Beggin' Strips Dog Snacks Review

Dog Loves 'em, what else is there to say? The resealable bag keeps the product fresh. We break up the Beggin Strips into smaller pieces and hide them around the house as the dog (Standard Poodle) seems to love the challenge of the hunt.
We pretty much limit the dog to only 2 of these a day so as to reduce spoiling her and any adverse health risks.
Great product as we have used these for years.

Product Description:
Beggin' Strips are dog treats that look, smell and taste like real bacon. Dogs will love the taste of these delicious snacks so much they will never know they are low in fat. We encourage you to use these treats when training your dog as a tasty reward. This treat is good for dogs of all ages. They are made with real meat and have the super-scrumptious, savory-flavor taste of bacon in every bite.
