An extremely talented actor, many are still waiting for Edward Norton to make his mark at the box office.  Norton sprang onto the Hollywood scene with a stunning and hard to forget intense performance in Primal Fear (Richard Gere). Also, an excellent performance in Fight Club (Brad Pitt), has kept Norton's name in front of fans.  Other popular Ed Norton movies include The Italian Job (Charlize Theron), Rounders (Matt Damon), and Kingdom of Heaven (Orlando Bloom ).   Ed Norton has appeared in over 15 movies.
