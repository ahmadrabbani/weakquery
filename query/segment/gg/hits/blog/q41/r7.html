SAG Awards 2011: Worst dressed

Published: Sunday, January 30, 2011, 9:39 PM     Updated: Monday, January 31, 2011, 8:36 AM
By Vicki Hyman/The Star-Ledger The Star-Ledger
Follow
Tweet
Share close

Enlarge Star-Ledger Wire Services Michael Pitt of "Boardwalk Empire" and model Jamie Bochert at the 2011 Screen Actors Guild Awards. MIKE NELSON/EPA SAG Awards 2011 red carpet worst dressed gallery (6 photos)

Newsflash: Strolling the red carpet at the 2011 Screen Actors Guild Awards, Helena Bonham Carter did not appear to have gotten dressed in the dark ... in the costume closet ... of Klown Kollege, so we'll have to make do with mocking a few other worthy contenders. Now "Boardwalk Empire" star Michael Pitt looked just fine from the chin down, but he sported the greasiest locks since ... Annette Bening at the Golden Globes. Melissa Leo of "The Fighter": Far too shiny. Angie Harmon of "Rizzoli and Isles": Far too frilly. Jenna Fischer of "The Office": Didn't you get a promotion on the show? Why are you still dressing like a secretary, albeit a real fancy one? "Rabbit Hole" star Nicole Kidman. Butt bow. 'Nuff said. And Lauren Velez, the "Dexter" detective? That gown is an indictable offense.
