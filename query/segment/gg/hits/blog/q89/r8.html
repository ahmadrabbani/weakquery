Mayo Clinic Creates Song Parody for Health Numbers

Mayo Clinic has created a parody of the hit 1982 song “867-5309” to help you learn your crucial health numbers.

Mayo staff, including doctors and nurses, are singing and dancing in this video. Their goal is to get you to learn your blood pressure, lipids, and body mass index.

The video will be unveiled today at the Mayo Clinic’s third annual Social Media Summit in Rochester.

Mayo Clinic hopes that soon the world will be singing along since the video will be available on YouTube.
