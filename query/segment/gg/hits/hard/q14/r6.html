Change doesn’t come about without resistance and conflicts. The goal is to provide media professionals with all necessary prerequisites to make balanced decisions in their work. If a journalist gets into conflict, trained lawyers should provide him with judicial assistance. For example, all Jordanian media organizations refused to support Jihad Momani, editor-in- chief of weekly Shihan, as he got fired for re-publishing the cartoons of the prophet Muhammad. CDFJ provided Momani with legal aid. Our partners and media professionals confirm the urgent need to improve the legal networks protecting journalists.  more
