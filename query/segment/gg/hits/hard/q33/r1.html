Law Enforcement - Working Dog (K-9) Procurement

SPECIAL BULLETIN:



Due to the enormous amount of inquiries and assistance by the public and law enforcement agencies around the United States, the Military Working Dog Foundation is temporarily suspending communications so that it can reply to the large amount of outstanding requests and to update our new website.

Please remember, the Military Working Dog Foundation is not a government agency, but rather a 501c3 non-profit. We have no funding or say whatsoever in what police department or other law enforcement agency receives a dog for patrol/detection duties via the U.S. Military's Working Dog Center's LE assistance program.

Our new online form for LE agencies and PD's will be online shortly.
