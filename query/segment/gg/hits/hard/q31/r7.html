Hotel Description:

The Three Gorges Project Hotel is located atthe construction site of Three Gorges Project Hotel, which is close to the Xiling Gorges. The hotel's unique design is in charming contrast with the elegant surrounding. The hotel offers five hundred guest rooms with different grades, all are well-equipped. One has only to gaze out the window to enjoy the view of the gorges and the Yangtze river. The western and Chinese restaurants offer Chinese and French style cuisine. The International Conference Center is the best place for holding conferences of all types. For entertainment, there is a gymnasium, a beauty salon, dancing hall, karaok, bowling alley and so on. The hotel operates under the philosophy of "People are our foundation and we constantly strive to be outstanding." Opened in 1997.

Hotel Facilities:
Business Center Laundry Currency Exchange Beauty Salon Sauna Restaurants in hotel Meeting Rooms Shopping Center Bar(s) Coffee Shop Health Club Chess Room Billiards Massage (Foot/Traditional/etc.) Ticket Service

Room Description:

224 standard rooms, 28 single rooms, and 22 luxurious suites. All rooms are equipped with air-conditioning, IDD, DDD, cable TV, and other facilities.
