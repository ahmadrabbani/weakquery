Olympic medalists Evan Lysacek and Sasha Cohen in town for private party

BY DEB PETERSON • dpeterson@post-dispatch.com > 314-340-8276 STLtoday.com | Posted: Monday, March 28, 2011 1:05 pm | Loading…

Font Size:
Default font size
Larger font size
  • Share
BY DEB PETERSON • dpeterson@post-dispatch.com > 314-340-8276
Sasha Cohen and Evan Lysacek

N"ICE" TIME: Kari Andersen, a stay-at-home mom in Webster Groves, has won a two-hour skating party and autograph session with Olympians Evan Lysacek and Sasha Cohen for her family and 25 guests tomorrow at the Webster Groves Ice Arena.

Andersen, 40, is the grand prize winner of the national Smucker's Skating Party contest, which is a promotion the company put on to commemorate the 25th anniversary tour of Smucker's Stars on Ice. The promotion ran from Dec. 20 through Feb. 18.

The party is the closest the stars will come to St. Louis on their tour, which will conclude April 9 in Bridgeport, Conn. St. Louis was not among the 25 cities on this year's tour, which began Nov. 28 in Lake Placid, N.Y.

Andersen has two sons: Luke, 8, and Elias, 5. She is married to Blake Andersen and says the skating party will include nine adults and 17 of Luke's friends from the Metro Edge Figure Skating Club, which skates at the Webster Groves rink.

The Smucker's Stars on Ice tour was founded in 1986 by skating icon Scott Hamilton. It has played more than 1,000 shows over the past 25 years and has won three Emmy awards and an ACE Cable Award for best sports special.
