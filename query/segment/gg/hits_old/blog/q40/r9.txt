( Hide copyright information )   Copyright    Dove Audio to release sci-fi audio drama series  NEWSTAR PUBLISHING has reached an agreement to release the Sci-Fi Channel's Seeing Ear Theatre, a series of science fiction full-cast audio dramatizations, on NewStar's Dove Audio imprint.  Launched two years ago, Seeing Ear Theatre until now has been available only at the Sci-Fi Channel's Web site, www.scifi.com. Visitors to the site listen to the productions using RealAudio, a free software program that streams audio in real time, so users don't have to download the sounds to their computers.
