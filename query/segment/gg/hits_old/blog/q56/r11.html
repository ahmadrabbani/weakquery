Teri Hatcher Movie

Momentum (2003)

Genre: Acttion, Drama, Thriller
Starring: Teri Hatcher, Louis Gossett Jr, Grayson McCouch, Michael Massee, Nicki Lynn Aycox
Director: James Seale

:::Synopsis
Playing in the ground of telekinetic talent, Momentum embarks on a similar plot to the feature Scanners. Zach Sheffard, a physics professor who possesses the amazing telekinetic power, is black mailed by former FBI agent Raymond Addison (Louis Gossett Jr.). Raymond makes the professor find the telekinetic gang, where Adrian Geiger (Michael Massee), his daughter Tristen Geiger (Nicki Aycox), and other telekinesists live in. Under this circumstances, Zach does what he is required to do, but later, he is contacted by another FBI agent, Jordan Ripps (Teri Hatcher), who tells him the dubious truth about his black mailer.
