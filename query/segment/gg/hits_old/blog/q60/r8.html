Re: 351w bell housing with T-5
Either the aluminum C5AA-6394-B bell or the cast iron C5TA-6394-A/D1TA-6394-AA bells will work with the T5 adapter plate. All the above are 164 tooth bells.
http://www.mustangtek.com/Bellhousin...lhousings.html
http://www.davidkeetoploaders.com/be...ingidchart.htm

Ebay usually has a few of the cast iron units selling.
Im not sure if a T5 bell will fit a 164 tooth flywheel.
